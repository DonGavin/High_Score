Our Story

About High Score

High Score's mission is to create a unique and welcoming environment where community, entertainment, and connection come together.

We strive to be Salem Virginia's premier destination for social events and family fun by offering engaging arcade experiences, quality food and beverages, and an atmosphere that brings people of all ages together.

Located in the heart of downtown Salem, we're walkable from Roanoke College and surrounded by the vibrant Main Street community. Whether you're a college student looking for a fun night out, a family seeking weekend entertainment, or a group planning a private event, High Score delivers an unforgettable experience.

Our self-pour tap system, rotating game selection, and data-driven approach ensure that every visit feels fresh and exciting. Join us for themed nights, tournaments, live music, and more. Welcome to Salem's gaming destination.
